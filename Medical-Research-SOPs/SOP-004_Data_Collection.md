# SOP-004: Data Collection

## Purpose

To ensure standardized, accurate, and ethical data collection throughout the research study.

## Scope

Applicable to all clinical, observational, laboratory, and public health research.

## Responsibilities

- Principal Investigator
- Co-investigators
- Research Assistants
- Data Collectors

---

## Procedure

### Step 1
Obtain IRB/Ethics approval before data collection.

### Step 2
Train all data collectors on study procedures.

### Step 3
Prepare all study materials:
- CRF
- Questionnaire
- Consent Form
- Study Log

### Step 4
Screen participants according to eligibility criteria.

### Step 5
Obtain written informed consent.

### Step 6
Assign a unique Participant ID.

### Step 7
Collect demographic information.

### Step 8
Collect clinical, laboratory, and study-specific variables.

### Step 9
Review each CRF for completeness before the participant leaves.

### Step 10
Store completed CRFs securely.

---

## Quality Control

✓ Complete every field

✓ Verify participant ID

✓ Use standard measurement procedures

✓ Correct errors with a single strike-through and initials

---

## Documents

- CRF
- Consent Form
- Screening Log
- Recruitment Log
- Laboratory Report
