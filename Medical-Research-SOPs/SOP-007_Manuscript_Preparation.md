# SOP-007: Manuscript Preparation

## Purpose

To prepare high-quality scientific manuscripts for publication.

## Scope

Applicable to all research publications.

---

## Procedure

### Step 1
Select the target journal.

### Step 2
Read the journal's author guidelines.

### Step 3
Prepare manuscript using IMRaD structure.

### Step 4
Write the Abstract.

### Step 5
Prepare tables and figures.

### Step 6
Check reporting guideline:

- STROBE
- CONSORT
- PRISMA
- CARE
- STARD

### Step 7
Check references.

### Step 8
Perform plagiarism screening.

### Step 9
Obtain final approval from all authors.

### Step 10
Prepare cover letter and submit.

---

## Checklist

✓ Title

✓ Abstract

✓ Keywords

✓ Introduction

✓ Methods

✓ Results

✓ Discussion

✓ Conclusion

✓ References

✓ Tables

✓ Figures
