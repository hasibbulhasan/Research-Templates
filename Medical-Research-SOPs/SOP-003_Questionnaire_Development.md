# SOP-003: Questionnaire Development

## Purpose

To develop reliable, valid, and standardized research questionnaires.

## Scope

Applicable to survey-based, observational, and clinical research.

## Procedure

### Step 1
Define study objectives.

### Step 2
Identify variables.

### Step 3
Review existing validated questionnaires.

### Step 4
Draft questionnaire items.

### Step 5
Obtain expert review.

### Step 6
Pilot test the questionnaire.

### Step 7
Assess validity and reliability.

### Step 8
Revise the questionnaire.

### Step 9
Finalize the questionnaire.

### Step 10
Archive the final version.

---

## Quality Checklist

✓ Simple language

✓ One concept per question

✓ Logical order

✓ Standard response options

✓ Pilot tested

---

## Documents Required

- Questionnaire Draft
- Expert Review Form
- Pilot Test Report
- Final Questionnaire
