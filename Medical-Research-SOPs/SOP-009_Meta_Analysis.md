# SOP-009: Meta-analysis

## Purpose

To conduct a statistically valid and reproducible meta-analysis.

## Scope

Applicable to quantitative systematic reviews.

---

## Responsibilities

- Principal Investigator
- Statistician
- Review Team

---

## Procedure

### Step 1
Confirm eligibility of included studies.

### Step 2
Extract quantitative outcome data.

### Step 3
Calculate effect sizes.

### Step 4
Assess heterogeneity:
- I² Statistic
- Chi-square Test

### Step 5
Select statistical model:
- Fixed-effect
- Random-effects

### Step 6
Perform pooled analysis.

### Step 7
Conduct subgroup analysis (if applicable).

### Step 8
Conduct sensitivity analysis.

### Step 9
Assess publication bias:
- Funnel Plot
- Egger's Test

### Step 10
Interpret findings.

---

## Recommended Software

- RevMan
- R (meta, metafor)
- Stata
- CMA

---

## Report

- Forest Plot
- Funnel Plot
- Risk of Bias Summary
- Summary of Findings
