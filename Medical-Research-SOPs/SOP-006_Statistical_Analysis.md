# SOP-006: Statistical Analysis

## Purpose

To ensure appropriate and reproducible statistical analyses.

## Scope

Applicable to all quantitative medical research.

---

## Procedure

### Step 1
Review study objectives.

### Step 2
Review Statistical Analysis Plan (SAP).

### Step 3
Import cleaned dataset.

### Step 4
Perform descriptive statistics.

### Step 5
Assess normality.

### Step 6
Choose appropriate statistical tests.

### Step 7
Perform regression analysis if required.

### Step 8
Interpret results.

### Step 9
Prepare tables and figures.

### Step 10
Document all analyses.

---

## Recommended Software

- SPSS
- R
- Stata
- SAS
- Jamovi

---

## Reporting

Report:

- Mean ± SD
- Median (IQR)
- Odds Ratio
- Relative Risk
- 95% Confidence Interval
- p-value
