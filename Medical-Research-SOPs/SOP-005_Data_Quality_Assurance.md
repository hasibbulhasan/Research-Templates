# SOP-005: Data Quality Assurance

## Purpose

To ensure research data are complete, accurate, consistent, and reliable.

## Scope

Applicable to all research datasets.

---

## Procedure

### Step 1
Review completed CRFs daily.

### Step 2
Double-check data entry.

### Step 3
Identify missing values.

### Step 4
Detect duplicate records.

### Step 5
Check data consistency.

### Step 6
Review outliers.

### Step 7
Verify unusual observations with source documents.

### Step 8
Document all corrections.

### Step 9
Lock the final dataset.

---

## Quality Indicators

- Completeness
- Accuracy
- Consistency
- Timeliness
- Validity
