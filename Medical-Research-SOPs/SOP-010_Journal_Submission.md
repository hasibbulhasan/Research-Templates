# SOP-010: Journal Submission

## Purpose

To ensure complete, accurate, and efficient submission of research manuscripts to peer-reviewed journals.

## Scope

Applicable to all journal submissions.

---

## Responsibilities

- Corresponding Author
- Co-authors

---

## Procedure

### Step 1
Select an appropriate target journal.

### Step 2
Review the journal's Author Guidelines.

### Step 3
Prepare all submission files:
- Manuscript
- Cover Letter
- Figures
- Tables
- Supplementary Files

### Step 4
Complete reporting guideline checklist:
- STROBE
- CONSORT
- PRISMA
- CARE
- STARD

### Step 5
Check references and formatting.

### Step 6
Run plagiarism screening.

### Step 7
Verify authorship information and ORCID IDs.

### Step 8
Submit the manuscript through the journal's online submission system.

### Step 9
Save the manuscript ID and confirmation email.

### Step 10
Monitor submission status and respond promptly to editorial or reviewer requests.

---

✓ Manuscript

✓ Cover Letter

✓ Reporting Guideline Checklist

✓ Figures

✓ Data Availability Statement
