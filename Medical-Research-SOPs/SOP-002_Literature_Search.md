# SOP-002: Literature Search

## Purpose

To ensure a systematic, transparent, and reproducible literature search before starting a research project.

## Scope

Applicable to all clinical, biomedical, public health, and nutrition research.

## Responsibilities

- Principal Investigator
- Research Team
- Librarian (if available)

---

## Procedure

### Step 1
Define the research question using:
- PICO
- PECO
- SPIDER (if applicable)

### Step 2
Identify keywords and synonyms.

### Step 3
Develop the search strategy using Boolean operators:
- AND
- OR
- NOT

### Step 4
Select databases:
- PubMed
- Scopus
- Web of Science
- Embase
- Cochrane Library
- Google Scholar

### Step 5
Apply inclusion and exclusion criteria.

### Step 6
Export citations.

### Step 7
Remove duplicate records.

### Step 8
Screen titles and abstracts.

### Step 9
Review full-text articles.

### Step 10
Document the complete search process.

---
