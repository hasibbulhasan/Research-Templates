# SOP-011: ORCID & Google Scholar Profile Management

## Purpose

To maintain an accurate, complete, and professional researcher profile through ORCID and Google Scholar.

---

## Scope

Applicable to all researchers, faculty members, postgraduate students, and research assistants.

---

## Responsibilities

- Individual Researcher
- Principal Investigator
- Research Coordinator (if applicable)

---

## Procedure

### Step 1: Create and Maintain ORCID

- Register for an ORCID iD.
- Verify your email address.
- Complete your profile information.
- Add employment and education history.

---

### Step 2: Maintain Google Scholar Profile

- Use your institutional email (if available).
- Add a professional profile photo.
- Include research interests.
- Keep affiliation updated.

---

### Step 3: Publication Management

- Review newly indexed publications.
- Add missing publications.
- Remove duplicate records.
- Correct author information where necessary.

---

### Step 4: Citation Monitoring

Review periodically:

- Total Citations
- h-index
- i10-index
- Citation Trends

---

### Step 5: Research Visibility

Link your publications with:

- ORCID
- GitHub
- ResearchGate (if used)
- Institutional Profile

---

### Step 6: Quality Check

Ensure:

✓ Correct title

✓ Correct author order

✓ Correct journal

✓ DOI available (if assigned)

✓ No duplicate publications

---

### Step 7: Annual Profile Review

Review and update your profiles at least once every year or after significant new publications.

---

## Best Practices

✓ Use the same author name across all publications.

✓ Use a consistent institutional affiliation.

✓ Link your ORCID where journals support it.

✓ Keep your research interests current.

✓ Maintain an up-to-date publication list.

---

## Do Not

✗ Create multiple Google Scholar profiles.

✗ Add publications that are not yours.

✗ Leave duplicate records uncorrected.

✗ Use inconsistent author names across publications.

---

## Documents

- ORCID Profile
- Google Scholar Profile
- Publication List
- DOI Records
- Curriculum Vitae (CV)

---

## References

Follow the guidance provided by ORCID, Google Scholar, and your institution regarding researcher profiles.
