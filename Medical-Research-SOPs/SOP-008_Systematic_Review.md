# SOP-008: Systematic Review

## Purpose

To conduct a transparent, reproducible, and high-quality systematic review following internationally accepted standards.

## Scope

Applicable to all systematic reviews in medicine, public health, nursing, nutrition, and allied health sciences.

---

## Responsibilities

- Principal Investigator
- Review Team
- Statistician (if applicable)

---

## Procedure

### Step 1
Develop a review protocol.

### Step 2
Define the research question using:
- PICO
- PECO
- SPIDER (if appropriate)

### Step 3
Register the protocol (e.g., PROSPERO, if eligible).

### Step 4
Develop a comprehensive search strategy.

### Step 5
Search multiple databases:
- PubMed
- Scopus
- Web of Science
- Embase
- Cochrane Library

### Step 6
Remove duplicate records.

### Step 7
Screen titles and abstracts independently by two reviewers.

### Step 8
Assess full-text eligibility.

### Step 9
Extract data using a standardized form.

### Step 10
Assess risk of bias using an appropriate tool.

### Step 11
Perform narrative synthesis or meta-analysis (if appropriate).

### Step 12
Prepare the PRISMA Flow Diagram.

### Step 13
Write the manuscript following PRISMA 2020.

---

## Required Documents

- Review Protocol
- Search Strategy
- Data Extraction Form
- Risk of Bias Assessment
- PRISMA Checklist
- PRISMA Flow Diagram
